  • Obtaining coodinates (video, English only)
  • Using Sky (video)

Note - Want to create compelling content for other Google Earth users? Learn how by following the Google Earth Outreach tutorials.

If you do not already have Google Earth installed, you can get the latest version here. Note that these tutorials describe how to use Google Earth Version 4 or later. To check the version you have, open Google Earth and click Help > About Google Earth. To see if there are any available updates, click Help > Check for Updates Online.

About Google Earth

With Google Earth, your computer becomes a window to anywhere on the planet, allowing you to view high-resolution aerial and satellite imagery, elevation terrain, road and street labels, business listings, and more. Learn more here.
